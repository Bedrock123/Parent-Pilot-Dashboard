Did you know Carlos is a
Learning Ally Hero?

Members like Carlos make our work at Learning Ally worth doing. Our team at Learning Ally has put this dashboard together to help celebrate Carlos’s
amazing progress in 2015.

Over the past year, Carlos read with Learning Ally for 362 days ...

totaling over 214 hours of reading!

Thank you, Carlos! You’re one of our Learning Heroes.
When Carlos reads with Learning Ally, they average over 1.2 hours of reading each day.
Reading builds critical vocabulary and comprehension skills. Students who read everyday for an hour will be exposed to over 5,000,000 words in a year!
Out of all those days spent reading, 120 were on the weekends!
Nationwide over 10,000,000 students could read like Carlos and get the same benefits of reading with Learning Ally
